Introduction

Welcome to Enroll-Me Market!

Our friendly llama will show you how to use the application in a few simple steps.

Your timetable

In Calendar view you'll see your current timetable.

Blue terms are those you can exchange.
Gray ones are fixed (usually lectures).

Introducing preferences

To let us find some exchanges for you, you have to tell us what your preferences are.

When you click one of the blue terms, you'll see what you can exchange it to. For your convenience, we only display the terms that don't conflict with your current timetable.

Introducing preferences

Red colour means you don't want to get this term...
Green colour marks the terms you want.

You can toggle the colors simply by clicking chosen terms. As simple as that!

Don't forget to save your preferences!
The only thing you have to do now is wait until we find somebody to exchage the term with you.

Available Now

Under some terms you'll see an 'Available Now' label. They're highlighted in orange

This means there's already someone who'd like to get your current term, and offers this one in exchange.

If you select it, the exchange will take place immediately - with a guarantee of success. Be careful though - there'll be no way to undo it!

Exchanges Summary

To keep track of your preferences, take a look at the Exchanges Summary. You can see a list of all the terms you'd like to get, as well as the terms that you've already exchanged.

An exchange can also take place when you're not logged in to the application. The list is a perfect way to check if there's some good news waiting for you!

That's all!

It seems you're ready for exchanging some terms. Good luck!

To find out more, head to the Q&A Section.

And if you need any help, feel free to contact us! We'll do our best to help you.
